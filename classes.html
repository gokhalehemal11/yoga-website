Yoga as a Therapy

Traditional Yoga

The pain & discomfort from Chronic ailments like Hypertension, Diabetes, Arthritis, Acidity, Asthma, Joint pain, Thyroid problems & Obesity can be gradually controlled by dedicated practice of Asanas, Bandhas, Mudras, Pranayamas, Shuddhikriyas along with few dietary modifications.

  • Specially planned schedules for – Teenagers, Pre-natal (pregnancy) & Post-natal periods after delivery), Menopause, Old age.
  • Special set of Pranayamas & Meditation to cope up with Stress, anxiety & Depression at all levels – school children, parents, Teachers, Corporate personnel.
  • Specially planned schedules for Senior Citizens.
A Perfect Blend of Yoga & Exercise

Power-Yoga

A chain of 30 yogic Asanas, Bandhas, Mudras & Pranayamas beautifully combined with gym exercises & Sun-salutations - performed in a rhythmic, synchronized manner with a specific breathing pattern.

  • Brings about the positive effects of both - “Exercise & Yoga” – perfect workout routine for today’s fast lifestyle.
  • Improves immunity, resistance power, flexibility, muscle tone, neuro - muscular co-ordination - "characteristics of "Yoga".
  • Develops confidence & brings about physical & mental relaxation.
